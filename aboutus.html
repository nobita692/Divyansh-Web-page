About Us

Alwar, known as the City of Parks, takes pride in its rich heritage and culture. MITRC, Alwar contributes to the vibrant essence of Alwar, Rajasthan. Established in 2007, Modern Institute of Technology & Research Centre (MITRC) is an esteemed Engineering and Management College that empowers students to excel and develop a robust personality. Renowned as one of the premier private engineering and management colleges in Alwar, Rajasthan, MITRC is nestled amidst the tranquil Aravali Hills on Alwar-Delhi-Tijara Highway.


The institute offers students cutting-edge infrastructure to enhance their understanding of engineering disciplines. Engineering plays a pivotal role in translating scientific breakthroughs into practical solutions for the betterment of society.
MITRC, Alwar is a magnet for aspiring engineers and management students seeking undergraduate and postgraduate programs such as B.Tech, M.Tech, and MBA. Our alumni have made significant contributions across diverse sectors, leveraging their knowledge to benefit industries, academia, research, and beyond.
MITRC Alwar provides students with unique internship opportunities and training programs focusing on the latest technologies. Our students have garnered prestigious accolades including Gold Medals in academics, Smart India Hackathon Grand Finale Winners, and triumphs in various state and national-level technical events.
Situated in the National Capital Region, our campus seamlessly blends urban accessibility with the natural allure of the Aravali Hills, offering a holistic residential experience with top-notch amenities like hostels, dining facilities, sports infrastructure, and recreational options.
What sets us apart from other colleges in Alwar are our commitment to academic excellence, distinguished faculty, industry collaborations, research prospects, modern amenities, alumni accomplishments, robust infrastructure, industry linkages, placement opportunities, disciplined environment, active participation in national and state-level technical competitions, and exceptional performance in exams like GATE and overall student satisfaction.
